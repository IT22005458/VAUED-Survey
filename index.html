Hi there! 👋

We are a 4-member final year team from the Faculty of Computing at SLIIT (Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology), currently working on our IT4031 – Visual Analytics & UX Design project.

We are building a Smart Garden Dashboard — a web app that helps home gardeners and small-scale farmers monitor their plants' health and decide the right time to water, all through simple, easy-to-read visuals.

This short survey helps us understand your preferences for charts, displays, and how information should be shown — so we can design something that truly works for you. There are no right or wrong answers. All responses are anonymous. 🙏

⚠️
This is an anonymous survey. No personal data is collected. Your responses will be used only for academic research purposes at SLIIT.
